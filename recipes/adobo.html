Adobong Baboy

Description

This is Adobong baboy (filipino-pork-adobo)

Widely known dish in the philippines

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Pepper
  • Oyster sause
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put the pork in medium heat until softens
  2. Put vinegar
  3. Put soy sauce
  4. Put pepper and oyster sauce
  5. Put salt
  6. Serve while hot
