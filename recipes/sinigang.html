Sinigang

Description

One of the best cuisine favorites

Sour and yummy

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Tamarind
  • Soy sauce
  • Kangkong
  • Patis
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put the pork in medium heat until softens
  2. Put water
  3. Put tamarind
  4. Put kangkong and soy sauce
  5. Put salt and patis
  6. Serve while hot
