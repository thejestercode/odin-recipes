SISIG

Description

One of the best Filipino favorites

Best in parties and inuman

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Onions
  • Soy sauce
  • Chili
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put the pork in medium heat until softens
  2. Fry the pork
  3. Chop the pork
  4. Add soy sauce
  5. Add chili and salt
  6. Serve while hot
